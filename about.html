Histon Global Ltd is a fully operational indegenous Oil & Gas consulting firm registered with the relevant regulatory bodies in Nigeria to operate as oil services contracting company in the design, procurement, installation, commissioning and maintenance and project management services to the upstream oil and gas industry.

Histon GLobal Ltd is a fast growing company in the provision of Fast and Reliable Procurement Support, Mechanical, Electrical, Instrumentation and Civil Facilities Installation, Repair and Maintenance to the Power, Marine, Petrochemical, Oil and Gas Industries in Nigeria, Histon Global Ltd directs its efforts to provide fast and courteous service by using product knowledge market expertise and total reliability.

Histon Global Ltd applies modern strategies & technologies for procurement sourcing and control management. Our understanding of the oil and gas industry and the perculiarity of its needs in terms of human and material resources gives us the upper edge in terms of quality, credibility, networking and experience.
